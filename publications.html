Journal Paper

Low-Light Image Enhancement Using CycleGAN-Based Near-Infrared Image Generation and Fusion.

Lee, M.-H.; Go, Y.-H.; Lee, S.-H.; Lee, S.-H. Mathematics 2024, 12, 4028.

Abstract

This research proposes a novel method for enhancing low-light images by generating and fusing near-infrared (NIR) images using a CycleGAN-based framework. The generated NIR images effectively capture details in dark environments, and their fusion with the original visible-light images results in significantly improved contrast and visibility.
