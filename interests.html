Interests

Computer Vision - HDR

Exploring techniques for High Dynamic Range imaging through image synthesis and enhancement to capture a wider range of light intensities, leading to more realistic and detailed images in challenging lighting conditions.

Embedded SW - RTOS & Communication

Focusing on Real-Time Operating Systems (RTOS) for predictable and timely execution in embedded systems. Also interested in various communication protocols to ensure reliable data transfer between devices like NVIDIA Jetson and Raspberry Pi.

Computational Optics & Holography

Passionate about the principles of wave optics and interference patterns, particularly in the context of digital holography and its potential to create true three-dimensional visual representations.

On-Device AI & Model Optimization

Investigating methods to deploy and optimize machine learning models for resource-constrained embedded devices, enabling real-time AI processing at the edge.
